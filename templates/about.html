About

Pull Up RPE helps you see if you are training hard enough. It gives you a RPE estimate based on how much slower your last pull up is compared to your first few.

Personally, my pull up numbers stalled after reaching sets of 10. I wanted a better way to estimate if I was training close to failure or not, and came across Jeff Nippard's video (see below) on using velocity loss to estimate RPE. Jeff uses video editing to determine rep times but after 3 minutes in iMovie I knew this method was probably worse than just using a stopwatch.

Pull Up RPE was built with Python, Flask, Javascript and uses Google's Video Intelligence API to analyze your pull ups.

Possible extensions
